ABOUT

It integrates molecular biology, genetics, and microbiology with cutting-edge technologies like genetic engineering and tissue culture. From producing life-saving vaccines and antibiotics to improving crop yields through genetically modified organisms, biotechnology plays a crucial role in addressing global challenges.
